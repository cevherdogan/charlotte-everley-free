Devon Home: Where Light Meets Legacy

This classic split-level retreat—poised at the peaceful meeting of Hickory and Contention—blends substance with sunlight. Renovated with intention, the granite mid-island anchors the kitchen while a secluded deck invites nature’s quiet chorus. HVAC, roof, and details done right.

Walkable to arboretum trails, parkland, and community life, this address isn’t just functional—it’s full of feeling. From Valley Creek’s hush to porches that wave back, Devon lives beautifully here.
