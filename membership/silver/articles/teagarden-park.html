Teegarden Park – A Quiet Retreat in Devon

Tucked behind Contention Lane just off Old State Road, Teegarden Park is a lesser-known haven where Devon’s families stretch out, slow down, and find rhythm in green space. Whether it’s Saturday morning t-ball or a quiet Tuesday dog walk, this hidden park shapes memories across generations.

🌳 What You'll Find

  • Field 4 – Renovated for Majors matchups and evening league games
  • Wooded walking paths that wrap behind the field perimeter
  • Benches shaded by tall oaks, perfect for spring picnics
  • A seasonal snack shed during youth tournaments

📍 Park Location

Address: 1210 Contention Ln, Devon, PA 19333
