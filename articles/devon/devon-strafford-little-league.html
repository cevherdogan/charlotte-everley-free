Devon–Strafford Little League

Where young families, old rivalries, and fresh-cut fields come together in the rhythm of spring and summer.

League Basics

Devon–Strafford Little League (DSLL) serves Easttown and Tredyffrin with divisions ranging from teeball to Majors. It’s a true neighbor-built league, powered by volunteers and supported by families.

  • Quickball & Teeball (K–1st Grade)
  • Coach-pitch (2nd–3rd Grade)
  • Kid-pitch through Majors (Ages 7–12)
  • Intermediate 50/70 for rising 13-year-olds

📍 Fields & Fabric of Community

Clarke Fields, situated at 145 Old State Road, welcomes Quickball, Teeball, and Coach-Pitch divisions. With easy access for early morning play and plenty of shaded sidelines, it’s the springtime hub for little league families.

Teegarden Park, just a few minutes up Old State Road toward Contention Lane, features the newly renovated Field 4—home to Majors-level games and all-star celebrations. Its proximity to the historic underpass adds scenic character to its community role.

🗺️ Interactive Map – Clarke & Teegarden Fields

Clarke Fields — 145 Old State Road, Berwyn (Devon Prep area) — is home to DSLL’s kinders and coach-pitchers. Easy parking, open lawns, and shaded sidelines make it ideal for younger families. This location is the heart of spring mornings and family gatherings.

Together, Clarke and Teegarden anchor Devon’s youth baseball circuit—Clarke nurturing the youngest players, and Teegarden bridging families and community moments across generations.

Volunteer Spirit

DSLL is a 100% volunteer-run 501(c)(3) supported by registration fees and community giving. Their recent renovation of Teegarden Field 4 totaled over $100,000, much of it crowdfunded via Venmo @DevonStraffordLL.

District Play & Community Culture

DSLL competes in District 27, with teams advancing to regionals every summer. But beyond wins and stats, it’s about community—sideline friendships, Saturday mornings, and memories passed down like gloves.

The grass may grow back each season, but the stories stay.
