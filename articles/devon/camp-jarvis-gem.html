Camp Jarvis & A Devon Gem

Where scouting tradition meets Main Line charm—nestled just beyond the picket fences of Devonshire Road.

📍 What Is Camp Jarvis?

Located just off Valley Forge Road, Camp Jarvis is a rustic log cabin retreat maintained by Devon Troop 50. It’s a gathering ground for scouts, families, and community groups to reconnect with nature year-round.

📅 Events & Seasons

From winter hikes to spring badge workshops, Camp Jarvis runs on a scout’s rhythm. Highlights include:

  • ❄️ Winter cabin retreats with Valley Forge walks
  • 🌱 Spring camping and fireside storytelling
  • 🍂 Autumn overnights and nature clean-up days

🏡 Nearby Real Estate Connection

Just minutes away lies 387 Devonshire Road, a rare FSBO opportunity offering Main Line elegance with a neighborly Devon address. Homes like this capture the same balance of privacy and connection Camp Jarvis embodies.

🤝 How to Participate

Scouting troops can book Camp Jarvis directly through Devon 50. Locals interested in supporting events or requesting community use can reach out via the Devon 50 group on Facebook.
